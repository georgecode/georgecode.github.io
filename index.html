George's guide to the best food in SF

This guide is meant for people who like good food in large portions, if you consider yourself a foodie this site may not be for you. Food is primarily judged on taste serving size, and how quick they make your food. Atmosphere, food presentation, and staff friendliness are not taken into consideration.

Pizza

The Pizza Shop

The pizza at this place is the best in SF hands down. I consider myself a pizza snob/expert ! I almost gave up on San Francisco pizza after trying so many bad pizza places in the city, but alas this place met my NY/NJ pizza standards, and my pants have gone up a size as proof.

Things to try

  • Plain slice
  • Plain slice (It's that good!)
  • Pepperoni slice
Pros Cons
At bus stop Parking
Super fast Cash only
Close to my house No delivery

Burrito

Taqueria Cancun

There’s so many great Mexican places in SF, Taqueria Cancun makes my best food in SF list because of their great green sauce, short line, huge serving, and great prices

Things to try

  • The green sause
  • Chicken burrito
  • Egg burrito
Pros Cons
Fast Lots of bums in the area
Easy parking No delivery
Cheap Cash Only

Asian Fusion

The Spice Jar

There’s also so many great Asian places in SF, The Spice Jar makes my list because of their delicious lettuce cups. I also like how they have silverware on the tables, so I don’t have to be embarrassed about not being able to use chopsticks.

Things to try

  • Lettuce cup
  • Wok Stir Seasonal Vegetables
  • Spiced Chicken Salad
Pros Cons
Not too salty Longer wait
Close to my house Parking
Has silverware A bit pricey
